Week View

  • Swipe Up: None
  • Swipe Left: Goes to next week (not animated)
  • Swipe Right: Goes to previous week (not animated)
  • Swipe Down: Menu
  • Tap Center Square: Today

Menu

  • Swipe Up: Week
  • Swipe Left: None
  • Swipe Right: None
  • Swipe Down: None
  • Tap Jump To Today: Today
  • Tap Jump to date: Month Picker

Today View

  • Swipe Up: Scroll Events
  • Swipe Left: Previous Day (Not Animated)
  • Swipe Right: Next Day (Not Animated)
  • Swipe Down: Week
  • Long Press on Event: Delete with confirm
  • Tap FAB: Event Details

Event Details View

  • Swipe Up: scroll
  • Swipe Left: None
  • Swipe Right: Cancel
  • Swipe Down: Scroll, if at top, back & save
  • Tap Name or Location: typing
  • Tap Date: Month Picker
  • Tap Time: Time Picker

Typing

  • Swipe down: Scroll, if at top save & exit
  • Swipe up: Scroll

Month Picker

  • Tap FAB: day picker

Day Picker

  • Tap FAB: year picker

Year Picker

  • Tap FAB: return to screen before month picker

Time Picker

  • Tap FAB: return to previous screen
