FlyBy

FlyBy is a simple app with a simple purpose - simplifying adding events to your calendar. I hardly ever used the calendar app on my phone, when I see a flyer for an event, I take a picture of it. This app, takes this picture, and adds the event directly to the calendar.

I'll admit, FlyBy doesn't look like much, but it uses Google Cloud APIs to parse text from images, and text analysis to try and determine what that text represents. Where that fails, I use regex and a bit of guesswork to fill in the gaps.

Of course, the user still has the option to make any changes before they submit.

Just take a picture, and add the event. A simple problem - a simple solution
