Over the Moon!

Over the Moon! is an app designed to allow climbers to catalog and share bouldering problems on system walls, which are walls with a high density of holds, and no predetermined routes. The app was designed to allow users to upload pictures of their wall, and create the route within the app.

I started by designing this as a website, this was one of the first sketches I made for the project. Eventually, as I began implementing my design I decided that Over The Moon should be developed as a mobile app, rather than a website. Even later, I decided that it should be neither, and should instead just be a notebook and a pen that can be kept next to the wall.

This is a very keen reminder in my memory that it can be good to start by thinking simple, and expand from there.

After making the paper sketches, I created each page as a wireframe in NinjaMock. and gave them functionality here on Invision.

I decided to do some user testing. When I asked people to find a problem and add it to their favorites, they were unable to do so, I had forgotten to add a page with a favorited item. I also found that everything generally a bit too condensed on the original mockups, making buttons harder to click. I fixed that by removing some unnecessary items.

I re-made every page in Sketch, this time focusing on fixing all the issues that arose during my interviews. As an aside, at some point I realized that I didn't like my font, but I found a Sketch plugin to let me find and replace fonts.
